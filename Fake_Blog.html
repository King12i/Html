Jordan joined the Bulls in 1984 as the third overall draft pick,
and quickly emerged as a league star and entertained crowds with his prolific scoring.
His leaping ability, demonstrated by performing slam dunks from the free throw line in Slam Dunk Contests,
earned him the nicknames Air Jordan and His Airness.
